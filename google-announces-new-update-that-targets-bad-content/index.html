Google announces new update that targets bad content

Maudy T. Pedrão

Google's official blog announced this afternoon that it has started a new algorithm update for its search engine, with a focus on content quality.

In Google's blog post, it says that:

Update starts next week and should take two weeks to finish completely.

The objective is to clean from the search results pages that will be considered bad, of low quality or that do not present answers to the users of the search engine of the American giant.

The update starts first in English and for other languages in the near future.

Google will let us know on its official channels.
